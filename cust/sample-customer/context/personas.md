## Customer Team

### Dr. Patricia Morales
**CEO, Acme Healthcare System**
- **Department:** Executive Leadership
- **Role in Engagement:** Executive Sponsor, Budget Approver
- **Priorities:** Patient outcomes, operational efficiency, cost reduction, regulatory compliance
- **Notes:** Physician background (cardiology), strong advocate for technology innovation. Appointed CEO in 2019, led the organization through COVID response. Champions data-driven decision making.
- **Communication Style:** Direct, focused on outcomes, prefers executive summaries with clear ROI metrics
- **Key Quote:** "If technology doesn't improve patient care or reduce costs, we shouldn't be investing in it."

### David Chen
**Chief Technology Officer**
- **Department:** Information Technology
- **Role in Engagement:** Technical Executive Sponsor, Platform Modernization Lead
- **Priorities:** Cloud migration strategy, infrastructure modernization, security & compliance, vendor consolidation
- **Notes:** Joined Acme in 2022 from Mayo Clinic. Strong Azure background, but pragmatic about multi-cloud. Focused on reducing technical debt and improving developer experience.
- **Communication Style:** Analytical, detail-oriented, appreciates technical depth but wants business context
- **Key Quote:** "We need a platform that scales with our ambitions, not one that holds us back."

### Dr. Rajesh Patel
**Chief AI Officer**
- **Department:** Clinical Innovation & AI
- **Role in Engagement:** AI/ML Platform Lead, Clinical Use Case Champion
- **Priorities:** Clinical decision support, AI governance, research partnerships, patient safety
- **Notes:** Former Stanford researcher, joined 2023. Built AI ethics framework. Cautious but progressive on AI adoption. Strong collaboration with Dr. Morales.
- **Communication Style:** Thoughtful, evidence-based, concerned with ethical implications and clinical validation
- **Key Quote:** "AI must augment physician judgment, never replace it. Patient safety is non-negotiable."

### Sarah Mitchell
**VP Engineering**
- **Department:** Software Engineering
- **Role in Engagement:** Developer Productivity Lead, GitHub Copilot Champion
- **Priorities:** Developer velocity, code quality, CI/CD modernization, talent retention
- **Notes:** 8 years at Acme, former team lead at Epic. Built current engineering culture. Strong advocate for modern development practices. Skeptical of vendor lock-in.
- **Communication Style:** Collaborative, metrics-driven, protective of developer experience
- **Key Quote:** "Our developers are our most valuable asset. If a tool doesn't make them more productive or happier, it's not worth it."

### Marcus Rivera
**Senior Director, Platform Infrastructure**
- **Department:** Infrastructure & Cloud Operations
- **Role in Engagement:** Technical Lead for Cloud Migration, Architecture Decision Maker
- **Priorities:** System reliability, security compliance, hybrid cloud strategy, cost optimization
- **Notes:** 12-year Acme veteran, led AWS migration in 2019. Expert in healthcare compliance (HIPAA, SOC2). Values proven technology over bleeding edge.
- **Communication Style:** Pragmatic, risk-averse, detailed technical questioner
- **Key Quote:** "Uptime is everything in healthcare. Every architecture decision must prioritize reliability and compliance."

### Lisa Zhang
**Principal Architect, Data Platform**
- **Department:** Data Engineering & Analytics
- **Role in Engagement:** AI Platform Technical Lead, Data Strategy Advisor
- **Priorities:** Data governance, real-time analytics, ML infrastructure, privacy controls
- **Notes:** PhD Computer Science, 5 years at Acme. Built current data lake on Snowflake. Expertise in healthcare data standards (FHIR, HL7).
- **Communication Style:** Technical expert, solution-oriented, curious about new technologies
- **Key Quote:** "Data is our strategic asset. The platform must enable innovation while protecting patient privacy."

### James Wilson
**Development Team Lead**
- **Department:** Application Development
- **Role in Engagement:** GitHub Copilot Pilot Lead, Developer Advocate
- **Priorities:** Code quality, team productivity, developer onboarding, technical debt reduction
- **Notes:** 3 years at Acme, previously at fintech startup. Strong React/Java expertise. Leads 12-person team building patient portal.
- **Communication Style:** Enthusiastic, hands-on, focused on practical developer impact
- **Key Quote:** "Copilot has already saved us 20% on development time. We need to scale this across all teams."
