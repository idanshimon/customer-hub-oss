## Overview

Acme Healthcare is one of the largest integrated healthcare delivery systems in the United States, serving over 2.8 million patients across 12 hospitals and 85 outpatient clinics throughout Texas and New Mexico. Founded in 1952, Acme has built a reputation for clinical excellence and innovation, recently ranked #3 in U.S. News & World Report's Best Hospitals in the Southwest region.

With approximately 15,000 employees including 3,200 physicians and 8,500 nurses, Acme operates a complex technology ecosystem that supports everything from electronic health records (Epic) to advanced imaging systems and clinical research platforms. The organization is currently in the midst of a comprehensive digital transformation initiative, driven by CEO Dr. Patricia Morales and CTO David Chen, aimed at modernizing their infrastructure, improving patient outcomes, and reducing operational costs by 15% over the next three years.

Acme's technology strategy centers on three core pillars: cloud migration from legacy on-premises systems, implementation of AI/ML solutions for clinical decision support and operational efficiency, and enhancement of developer productivity through modern DevOps practices. The organization has committed $45M over 24 months to this transformation, with Microsoft emerging as a strategic partner alongside their existing AWS infrastructure.
