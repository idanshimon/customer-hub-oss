## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

#### AWS (Embedded - Coexistence Strategy)
- **Strength:** 70% of current workloads, deep Epic integration, team expertise
- **Weakness:** Limited AI/ML healthcare solutions, higher costs for AI workloads
- **Our Position:** Azure as strategic secondary cloud for AI/ML and modern workloads
- **Status:** Not directly competing for displacement, focusing on net new AI workloads

#### Google Cloud Platform (Evaluating - Displacement Strategy)  
- **Strength:** Healthcare AI reputation (Med-PaLM), competitive pricing on AI/ML
- **Weakness:** Limited healthcare customer base, integration complexity
- **Our Position:** Better healthcare compliance story, existing Microsoft relationship
- **Status:** Under evaluation for AI platform, not currently preferred

#### Cursor/Codeium (Active - Direct Competition)
- **Strength:** Developer preference in some teams, competitive pricing  
- **Weakness:** Enterprise governance gaps, limited integration with Microsoft stack
- **Our Position:** Enterprise-grade security, GitHub integration, organizational adoption
- **Status:** Direct threat to Copilot expansion, addressed through pilot metrics
